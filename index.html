A. P. J. Abdul Kalam

1931 -2015

Missile Man of India

A.P.J. Abdul Kalam was an Indian scientist and the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007. Known as the "Missile Man of India," he made significant contributions to India's space and defense programs, including developing India's first indigenous satellite launch vehicle and playing a key role in the country's nuclear tests. Born in Rameswaram in 1931, he was a respected figure known for his simple lifestyle and inspiring work.
